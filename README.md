# Falling Behind 🦆

**Falling Behind** is a web application designed to encourage users to stay active by tracking walking goals, monitoring progress, and discovering new places to walk. Built during **Technica**, the project combines goal tracking, achievement systems, and location-based recommendations to make walking more engaging and rewarding.

## Inspiration

Many people struggle to stay consistent with physical activity, especially when walking can feel repetitive or unmotivating. We wanted to create a tool that helps users build healthier habits by setting goals, visualizing progress, and exploring new walking destinations in their area.

## Features

### 🎯 Goal Tracking
- Set personalized walking distance goals
- Track progress toward goals over time
- View completed and ongoing goals

### ⏱️ Walk Monitoring
- Log walking sessions
- Record distance traveled and time spent walking
- Monitor activity history

### 🏅 Achievement Badges
- Earn badges for reaching milestones
- Stay motivated through progress-based rewards
- Track achievements and accomplishments

### 🗺️ Location-Based Recommendations
- Discover nearby parks, trails, and points of interest
- Search for walking destinations using an interactive map
- Explore new places to walk and stay active

### 📊 Progress Dashboard
- Visualize walking statistics
- Track cumulative distance and activity trends
- Monitor overall progress toward fitness goals

## How It Works

1. Set a walking goal based on your desired distance.
2. Log your walks and track the distance and time completed.
3. View your progress toward your goals through the dashboard.
4. Earn achievement badges as you reach milestones.
5. Use the interactive map to discover nearby places to walk.
6. Stay motivated by tracking your growth and accomplishments.
